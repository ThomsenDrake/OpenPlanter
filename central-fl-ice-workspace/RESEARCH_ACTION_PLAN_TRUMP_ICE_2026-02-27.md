3. Extract from ICE stats:
   - Orange County population for each year (FY2022-FY2026)
   - Use metric columns to estimate daily average

**Calculation Template:**
```
FY2024:
- Average daily population: [FROM ICE STATS]
```

**What to Do:**
1. Prepare FOIA request to ICE for transfer records

   - Facilities: Hillsborough Falkenburg, Seminole JEP, Polk, Volusia Branch, Volusia Correctional

2. Use ICE FOIA online portal
   - URL: https://www.ice.gov/foia/request
   
3. FOIA Request Template:
```
Request under Freedom of Information Act (5 U.S.C. § 552):

I request records regarding detainee transfers from the following facilities with 287(g) 
agreements in Florida's I-4 corridor for the period January 1, 2025 to February 27, 2026:

1. Hillsborough County Falkenburg Road Jail
2. John E. Polk Correctional Facility (Seminole County)
3. Polk County Jail
4. Volusia County Branch Jail
5. Volusia County Correctional Facility

Specifically, I request:
- Number of immigration detainees transferred from each facility
- Transfer destination facilities
- Date of transfer
- Reason for transfer
- Length of detention before transfer

Format: Electronic records preferred (Excel or CSV).

Fee category: Educational/News media (select appropriate)
```

**Expected Output:**
- FOIA tracking number
- Estimated response date

**Time Estimate:** 1 hour
