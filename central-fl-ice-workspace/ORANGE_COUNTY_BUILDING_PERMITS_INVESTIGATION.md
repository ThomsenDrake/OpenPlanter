## Federal Exemption from Local Permits

**Critical Finding**: Federal projects are NOT subject to local regulations, including:
- Building permits
- Zoning restrictions
- Local review processes

**Implications**:
1. ICE/GSA may not need to file permits with Orange County
2. Federal agencies can bypass local permitting entirely
3. Construction may proceed without public record in county database
4. Only federal procurement records (SAM.gov, USAspending.gov) would show activity
