# Orlando ICE Processing Center - Federal Procurement Investigation

**Investigation Complete | February 27, 2026**

---

## 📋 What Was Investigated

Crawled federal procurement sites for references to the **Orlando ICE Processing Center** - a planned 1,500-bed immigration detention facility at 8660 Transport Drive, Orlando, FL.

---

## 🎯 Key Finding

**NO federal contract has been awarded yet.** ICE is in the **exploratory/acquisition phase** for converting the Beachline Logistics Center warehouse into a detention facility. This is the optimal time for monitoring and potential intervention in the procurement process.

## 🎓 Key Learnings

### ICE Procurement Strategy
1. Uses **GSA** for real estate acquisition
2. May use **8(a) program** to bypass competition for contracts <$4.5M
3. **Tribal entities** being used for rapid procurement
4. **Extreme secrecy** around acquisition details

### Red Flags Identified
1. Property listings removed before public announcement
2. GSA refuses to clarify role
3. 9-month timeline is aggressive
4. Facility not zoned for habitation
5. Congressional opposition could trigger oversight
