## Request Overview

Pursuant to Florida's Public Records Law (Chapter 119, Florida Statutes) and the Sunshine Law, I hereby request access to public records related to the Scout microtransit program in Seminole County, operated under contract with BeFree LLC (d/b/a Freebee). This request concerns all records related to the planning, procurement, contract award, implementation, and operation of the Scout microtransit service.

---

## Specific Records Requested

### 1. PROCUREMENT AND RFP DOCUMENTS

**RFP 604918-25/PCD - Micro-Transit Services for Seminole County:**

- Complete RFP document including all addenda, amendments, and Q&A responses
- All vendor proposals submitted in response to RFP 604918-25/PCD, including:
  - BeFree LLC (d/b/a Freebee) complete proposal
  - Circuit Transit complete proposal
  - River North Transit complete proposal
- Evaluation criteria, scoring matrices, and evaluation committee member assignments
- All evaluation sheets, scoring documents, and rankings of proposals
- Selection committee meeting minutes, notes, and deliberation records
- Presentations made by vendors to evaluation committee or Board
- Reference check documentation and due diligence materials
- Cost-benefit analyses comparing vendor proposals

### 2. CONTRACT DOCUMENTS

**Contract between Seminole County and BeFree LLC:**

- Fully executed contract with all signature pages
- All contract amendments, modifications, or change orders
- Scope of work documents and service level agreements
- Insurance and bonding documentation
- Performance metrics and key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Pricing schedules, rate cards, and payment terms
- Vehicle specifications and fleet requirements
- Service zone definitions and boundary maps
- Termination clauses and renewal terms

### 3. MEETING MINUTES AND RECORDINGS

**Board of County Commissioners (BCC) Meetings:**

- Meeting minutes, agendas, and backup materials for all BCC meetings where the Scout microtransit program was discussed from June 2024 to present, including but not limited to:
  - May 20, 2025 BCC meeting (contract award)
  - April 2025 BCC meetings (policy direction)
  - Any workshops, work sessions, or special meetings on microtransit
- Audio and/or video recordings of these meetings
- Presentation materials and slide decks from county staff
- Public comments and correspondence related to the program

### 4. PLANNING AND ANALYSIS DOCUMENTS

**Program Planning Records:**

- Internal memoranda, emails, and correspondence regarding the decision to replace LYNX fixed-route service
- Cost analyses documenting the increase from $7 million (2019) to $17 million (2025) for LYNX service
- Ridership studies and analyses for LYNX routes in Seminole County
- Research on microtransit models (including Arlington, Texas case study)
- Feasibility studies or needs assessments
- Community input and stakeholder engagement records
- Coordination records with LYNX/Central Florida Regional Transportation Authority
- Meeting notes from consultations with local municipalities (November-December 2024)

### 5. FINANCIAL RECORDS

**Contract Expenditures and Payments:**

- All invoices submitted by BeFree LLC to Seminole County from contract execution to present
- Payment records showing amounts paid to BeFree LLC
- Budget documents and appropriations for the Scout program
- Comparison of actual costs vs. projected costs for both Scout and former LYNX service
- Cost per ride calculations and analyses
- Revenue and fare collection records
- Any grants, subsidies, or external funding sources for the program

### 6. PERFORMANCE AND OPERATIONAL DATA

**Service Performance Metrics:**

- Monthly or quarterly performance reports submitted by BeFree LLC
- Ridership data by month, zone, and time period (October 2025 to present)
- Response time metrics (average pickup times, wait times)
- Vehicle utilization rates and fleet deployment reports
- Customer satisfaction surveys or feedback
- Service reliability metrics (on-time performance, completed rides)
- Complaint logs and resolution records
- Service level adjustments and demand-response documentation
- Any performance deficiencies or contract violations documented

### 7. COMMUNICATIONS

**Correspondence Related to Scout Program:**

- Emails between county staff and BeFree LLC representatives
- Communications between procurement staff and vendors during RFP period
- Correspondence with LYNX regarding service transition
- Communications with other government agencies about the program
- Public inquiries and county responses regarding the Scout service

### 8. SERVICE DESIGN DOCUMENTS

**Program Implementation:**

- Service zone boundary maps and rationale for zone definitions
- Vehicle branding and marketing materials (Scout branding)
- Mobile app development and technology platform documentation
- Driver training and certification requirements
- Safety protocols and incident reporting procedures
- Accessibility compliance documentation (ADA)
- Marketing and outreach materials

## Additional Notes

This request is based on publicly available information indicating that:

1. Seminole County issued RFP 604918-25/PCD on December 11, 2024 for microtransit services
2. The Board of County Commissioners awarded a contract to BeFree LLC on May 20, 2025
3. The Scout service launched in October 2025, replacing most LYNX fixed-route service
4. The program involves significant public expenditure (reported range of $4.8M-$9.2M in vendor proposals)
5. The program represents a major shift in public transit delivery in Seminole County

Public disclosure of these records will promote transparency and accountability regarding the expenditure of taxpayer funds on a major public transit program.
