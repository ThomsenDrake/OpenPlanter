## HIGH-PRIORITY FOIA TARGETS (Start Here)

### 1. **IGSA Contracts** (Limitations #3, #12, #14)
**Fastest Path: Florida Sunshine Law**
- **Submit to:** 6 County Sheriff's Offices (Hillsborough, Pinellas, Polk, Orange, Seminole, Volusia)
- **Request:** IGSA agreements, amendments, reimbursement rates, financial records
- **Timeline:** 10-30 days
- **Success Likelihood:** 90%
- **Why:** State law faster than federal FOIA, fewer exemptions

**Parallel Federal Track:**
- **Submit to:** ICE Procurement Office
- **Request:** Same IGSA documents
- **Timeline:** 30-45 days
- **Purpose:** Backup if state requests denied; federal oversight angle

---

### 2. **ICE Facility Codes** (Limitation #1)
- **Submit to:** ICE Enforcement and Removal Operations (ERO)
- **Request:** "All ICE facility identification codes for Florida I-4 corridor facilities"
- **Timeline:** 20-30 days
- **Success Likelihood:** 95%
- **Why:** Administrative data, minimal exemption risk

---

### 3. **287(g) Program Records** (Limitations #8, #14)
- **Submit to:** ICE 287(g) Program Office
- **Request:** MOA amendments, compliance reports, training records, financial allocations
- **Timeline:** 30-45 days
- **Success Likelihood:** 85%
- **Why:** Public program, oversight documents routinely released

**Parallel State Track:**
- **Submit to:** County Sheriff's Offices (Florida Sunshine Law)
- **Request:** 287(g) activity reports, funding records
- **Timeline:** 10-30 days

---

### 4. **Facility Inspection Reports** (Bonus Discovery)
- **Submit to:** ICE Office of Detention Oversight (ODO)
- **Request:** All inspection reports, compliance reviews, corrective action plans (2020-2026)
- **Timeline:** 30-45 days
- **Success Likelihood:** 80%
- **Why:** ODO reports often in FOIA library; quality metrics valuable

---

## MODERATE-PRIORITY FOIA TARGETS (Submit After Tier 1)

### 5. **Historical Population Data** (Limitation #6)
- **Submit to:** ICE ERO + Office of Immigration Statistics
- **Request:** Daily/monthly population statistics (2019-2026)
- **Timeline:** 30-60 days
- **Success Likelihood:** 70%
- **Challenge:** May claim privacy exemption for individual-level data
- **Strategy:** Request aggregate statistics only

---

### 6. **Orlando Facility Procurement** (Limitation #7)
- **Submit to:** ICE Acquisition + GSA Public Buildings Service
- **Request:** Site assessments, market research, procurement planning documents
- **Timeline:** 30-45 days
- **Success Likelihood:** 60%
- **Challenge:** May claim "pre-decisional" exemption
- **Strategy:** Request segregable factual data (site visits, capacity assessments)

---

### 7. **Transfer Pattern Data** (Limitation #10)
- **Submit to:** ICE ERO
- **Request:** Detainee transfer records (origin/destination) from I-4 corridor facilities
- **Timeline:** 30-60 days
- **Success Likelihood:** 65%
- **Challenge:** Privacy exemption for individual records
- **Strategy:** Request "statistical summary of transfer destinations"

---

### 8. **ICE-Dedicated Capacity** (Limitation #4)
- **Submit to:** ICE ERO + County Sheriffs
- **Request:** ICE-dedicated bed allocations vs. total capacity
- **Timeline:** 20-45 days
- **Success Likelihood:** 75%
- **Challenge:** May claim "operational security"
- **Strategy:** County records more accessible under state law

---

## LOW-PRIORITY / NOT FOIA-ABLE

### 9. **Real-Time Population Data** (Limitation #11)
- **Status:** FOIA not suitable (retrospective only)
- **Alternative:** Establish recurring FOIA pipeline for weekly/monthly updates

### 10. **Adjacent County Facilities** (Limitation #9)
- **Status:** Outside current scope, but FOIA-able
- **Priority:** TIER 3 (expand scope strategically later)

### 11. **Planned Facilities (Pre-Procurement)** (Limitation #13)
- **Status:** No records exist until procurement begins
- **Strategy:** Monitor SAM.gov for solicitations, then submit FOIA

## MULTI-AGENCY REQUEST STRATEGY

### PHASE 1: State Law Requests (Month 1)
**Submit 6 parallel requests to County Sheriffs:**
1. Hillsborough County Sheriff's Office
2. Pinellas County Sheriff's Office
3. Polk County Sheriff's Office
4. Orange County Sheriff's Office
5. Seminole County Sheriff's Office
6. Volusia County Sheriff's Office

**Request:** IGSA contracts, 287(g) records, financial data, capacity allocations

**Advantages:**
- Faster response (10-30 days)
- Lower fees
- Fewer exemptions
- Attorney's fees for wrongful denial

---

### PHASE 2: Federal FOIA Requests (Month 1-2)
**Submit 7 parallel requests to federal agencies:**

1. **ICE Enforcement and Removal Operations (ERO)**
   - Facility codes, population data, transfer records, capacity data

2. **ICE Office of Detention Oversight (ODO)**
   - Inspection reports, compliance reviews

3. **ICE 287(g) Program Office**
   - MOA documents, compliance reports, training records

4. **ICE Procurement/Acquisition Office**
   - IGSA contracts, Orlando facility procurement

5. **DHS Privacy Office**
   - Escalation point if ICE denies

6. **GSA Public Buildings Service**
   - Orlando facility property assessments

7. **Office of Immigration Statistics (OIS)**
   - Historical detention statistics

---

### PHASE 3: Follow-Up and Appeals (Month 3-6)
- File administrative appeals for denials
- Prepare litigation strategy
- Submit additional targeted requests based on initial responses
