### #3: 287(g) Over-Implementation ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Key Finding:** Many agencies signed beyond legal requirements

**Required:** ONLY jail operators, and only ONE model (could choose narrowest WSO)

## New Intel: Rebooking Practice

**What ICE Was Doing:**
- Remove detainees every 72 hours
- Rebook under new number
- Reset 72-hour clock
- Prolong detention without due process
- Attorneys couldn't find clients

**Orange County Response:**
- Demings: "Circumvents intended operation of IGSA"
- **ENDED March 1, 2026**
- Practice may continue in other counties

---

## New Intel: BOA Alternative

**Basic Ordering Agreements:**
- For counties WITHOUT IGSA
- ICE pays $50 per immigrant (up to 48 hours)
- Effective rate: **$25/day**
- Compare to: Orange County $88/day IGSA

**29 Florida counties have BOAs (as of Feb 2019)**

**Orange County's Threat Analysis:**
- County claims BOA is "mandatory" if IGSA terminated
- **LEGAL ANALYSIS SAYS: VOLUNTARY**
- Real motive: Maximum reimbursement ($88 vs $25/day)
