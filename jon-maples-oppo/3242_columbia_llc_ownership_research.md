### Potential Lines of Attack:

**1. Landlord/Tenant Issues:**
- 3242 Columbia LLC filed eviction against tenant Amelia Jennings in July 2024
- Could be used to portray Maples as aligned with aggressive landlords
- Relevant if tenant rights or affordable housing are campaign issues

**2. Out-of-State LLC:**
- 3242 Columbia LLC formed in Wyoming, not Florida
- Wyoming LLCs often used for anonymity and asset protection
- Could portray donation as coming from opaque out-of-state entity

**3. Wealthy Donor Narrative:**
- Bols is a multi-millionaire real estate investor
- $10,000 from one LLC represents potential special interest influence
- Could contrast with Maples's "working class roots" messaging

**4. California Lawsuit Connection:**
- Bols sued Gov. Newsom over COVID restrictions
- Could link Maples to COVID skepticism/denialism
- May resonate in post-pandemic political environment

**5. Multiple LLC Structure:**
- Bols controls 8+ LLCs, potentially obscuring assets
- Could raise questions about transparency
- Typical of sophisticated real estate investors but may appear sketchy to average voters

### Mitigating Factors:

1. **Legal Donation:** No evidence of illegality
2. **Same Party:** Both are Republicans (expected support)
3. **Local Business:** Bols operates in Palm Beach County (not truly out-of-state interests)
4. **Public Record:** LLC ownership is transparent in Florida corporate filings
5. **No Clear Quid Pro Quo:** No obvious policy request or favor
