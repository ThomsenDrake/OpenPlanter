### WHAT "FEDERAL GOVERNMENTAL DELEGATE" MEANS

**Likely Responsibilities:**
- Representing Northwestern Mutual in federal policy discussions
- Participating in NAIFA Congressional Conference fly-ins to Washington D.C.
- Engaging in grassroots advocacy (mobilizing others rather than direct lobbying)
- Communicating with elected officials about insurance/financial issues
- Potential involvement in NMPAC activities

**Why Not a Registered Lobbyist:**
- Under the Lobbying Disclosure Act, individuals must register if they:
  - Make more than one lobbying contact, AND
  - Spend >20% of their time on lobbying activities, AND
  - Receive income for lobbying
- Maples likely engages in activities below these thresholds
- Grassroots advocacy (educating and mobilizing) doesn't require registration
