## Florida Removal Cities

TRAC data shows departure points for deportations from Florida. The state filter shows:

**Primary Removal Processing Locations:**
- Miami Area (Krome SPC, Broward TC)
- Tampa Area (Hillsborough County Jail)
- Orlando Area (Orlando ICE Processing Center)

**Note:** TRAC's city-level data primarily shows Texas border ports of entry, as most deportations exit through land borders. Florida-based removals likely processed through:
- Miami International Airport
- Other Florida airports
- Transfer to Texas/Arizona border facilities

## Implications for I-4 Investigation

### 1. Due Process Concerns
- High percentage of non-criminal detainees (73.6%)
- Removals take significant time (data through Feb 2024)
- Detention surge may indicate processing bottlenecks

### 2. Facility Utilization
- I-4 corridor facilities house ~303 detainees (TRAC Feb 2026)
- This is a fraction of Florida's 5,231 total
- Most Florida detainees in South Florida facilities

### 3. Transparency Gaps
- City-level removal data not detailed for Florida
- I-4 corridor removal processing unclear
- Need FOIA data for local removal statistics

### 4. Contract Structure Impact
- USMS IGA facilities may have different removal timelines
- IGSA facilities may process faster/slower
- Financial implications of extended detention

---

## Data Limitations

1. **Removal data ends Feb 2024** - Does not capture recent surge
2. **No county-level removal data** - Can't isolate I-4 corridor specifically
3. **No facility-specific removal rates** - Can't compare efficiency
4. **No time-in-detention before removal** - Missing cost analysis
