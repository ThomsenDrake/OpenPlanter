## The $38 Billion Context

**ICE Detention Reengineering Initiative (DRI):**
- **Total Cost:** $38.3 billion
- **Funding:** "One Big Beautiful Bill Act" (Congress)
- **Goal:** 92,600 detention beds nationwide
- **Deadline:** November 30, 2026
- **Orlando Role:** One of 16 regional processing centers

**Facility Specifications:**
- **Capacity:** 1,500 detainees
- **Type:** Processing center (3-7 day holds)
- **Cost:** $99-100M purchase + renovation
- **Timeline:** Target activation November 2026
