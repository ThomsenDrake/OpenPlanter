# Orlando ICE Processing Center - Procurement Monitoring Action Plan

**Daily/Weekly Checklist | February 27, 2026**

---

## 🎯 Objective

Monitor the federal procurement process for the Orlando ICE Processing Center to:
1. Detect when solicitation is posted
2. Identify selected contractors
3. Track procurement timeline
4. Document potential irregularities
5. Support community opposition efforts

---

## Daily Monitoring Tasks

### ☐ SAM.gov Check (5 minutes)

**What to Monitor:**
1. Log into SAM.gov
2. Go to "Contract Opportunities"
3. Run saved searches:
   - "ICE detention Orlando"
   - "Orange County Florida detention"
   - NAICS Code: 561612
   - PSC Code: S206
4. Check for new opportunities posted in last 24 hours
5. Record any findings in monitoring log

**What to Look For:**
- New RFPs, RFIs, or Sources Sought notices
- Presolicitation notices for detention services
- Awards to GEO Group, CoreCivic, MTC, or other detention operators
- GSA Schedule orders for construction or security services

**If You Find Something:**
- Screenshot the posting immediately
- Download all attached documents
- Note solicitation number, response deadline, contact information
- Update team within 1 hour

---

### ☐ Property Records Check (2 minutes)

**What to Monitor:**
1. Go to Orange County Property Appraiser
2. Search for: 8660 Transport Drive Orlando FL 32832
3. Check for ownership changes
4. Verify current owner still listed as Beachline Logistics Center LLC

**What to Look For:**
- Change to "United States of America" as owner
- Change to "General Services Administration" as owner
- Sale price recorded (compare to $99M estimate)
- Sale date

**If Ownership Changes:**
- Document immediately with screenshots
- Notify team (ownership transfer = purchase imminent)
- Check for corresponding GSA or ICE press release

---

## Escalation Triggers

**Take Immediate Action If:**

1. **SAM.gov Solicitation Posted**
   - Download all documents within 1 hour
   - Analyze requirements and timeline
   - Share with community and journalists
   - Consider bid protest strategy

2. **Property Ownership Transfers**
   - Document sale price and date
   - Check for corresponding contract award
   - Update timeline with "purchase complete"

3. **Contract Award Announced**
   - Identify winning contractor
   - Research contractor history
   - Assess opportunities for challenge
   - Coordinate community response

4. **FOIA Denial**
   - File appeal immediately
   - Contact OGIS for mediation
   - Publicize denial as evidence of secrecy

5. **Major News Break**
   - Archive all coverage
   - Issue press release if appropriate
   - Coordinate community response
   - Update timeline and documentation
